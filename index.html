About GARS Robotics

Founded in 2019, we are on a mission to make industrial automation accessible, intelligent, and reliable.

About Us — GARS Robotics and Automation is a Bangalore-based robotic integration company specializing in industrial robot programming, commissioning, and automation support. We work with globally recognized robotic platforms such as ABB, FANUC, Kawasaki, and other leading automation systems. Our core expertise lies in ABB industrial robots.

Our expertise includes robotic cell integration, cycle time optimization, troubleshooting, preventive maintenance, and on-site technical support for manufacturing environments. We focus on delivering reliable and efficient automation solutions designed to improve productivity, accuracy, and operational safety. With a commitment to quality and long-term industrial support, GARS Robotics and Automation partners with manufacturing companies to enhance automation performance and reduce production downtime.

Our Mission

To provide reliable and efficient robotic programming and automation support that enhances productivity, reduces downtime, and strengthens manufacturing performance.

Our Vision

To become a trusted industrial automation partner known for high-quality integration, technical expertise, and long-term customer support.
